## Project Overview

This project analyzes two years of retail sales data (1997–1998) using Microsoft Power BI to evaluate customer behavior, product contribution, geographical performance, revenue growth, and business trends.

The analysis focuses on identifying major revenue drivers, product performance patterns, customer purchasing behavior, and operational insights that can support strategic business decisions.

## Business Objectives

The project was designed to answer key business questions such as:

1. Profit trends over time
2. Revenue growth performance
3. Geographical sales contribution
4. Purchasing power by income level
5. Occupation-based purchasing behavior
6. Return rate impact on revenue

## Tools & Techniques

* Microsoft Power BI
* Star Schema Data Modeling
* KPI Development
* Customer Segmentation
* Data Visualization
* Business Intelligence Reporting
* Trend & Performance Analysis

### Executive Dashboard

Key Metrics

* **Total Revenue:** $24.9M

* **Total Orders:** 25.5K
* **Return Rate:** 2.2%

Yearly Performance Insights

Further analysis revealed the following yearly trends:

| Year | Revenue | Profit |
| ---- | ------- | ------ |
| 2020 | $6.4M   | $2.6M  |
| 2021 | $9.3M   | $4.0M  |
| 2022 | $9.2M   | $3.9M  |

Key Observations

* Revenue declined slightly by **1.1% in 2022**
* Orders increased by **10% in 2022**
* Profit increased by **43.8%** from the starting year to the final sales year
* Total orders increased by **348.7%**
* Sales for 2022 ended in June, meaning the dataset only represents half-year performance


### Product Performance Analysis

This section evaluates product contribution, sales performance, and target achievement.
<img width="662" height="370" alt="Product_dashboard" src="https://github.com/Debbyjones99/Adventureworks_bike_shop-guided_project-/blob/main/Dashboard_screenshots/adventureworks_product_dashboard.png" />

Key Insights

* **Fender Set – Mountain** generated the highest revenue of **$87,041**
* The product achieved **1,975 orders**
* Monthly sales targets were not consistently met
* The highest single-day revenue generated was **$1,802 on 06/06/2022**
* **Water Bottle – 30 oz** generated:

  * **3,980 orders**
  * **$39,755 revenue**

Product Insight

The analysis revealed that **higher order quantity does not always translate to higher revenue generation**, showing the importance of pricing strategy and product value contribution.
