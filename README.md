## Project Overview

This project analyzes two years of retail sales data (1997–1998) using Microsoft Power BI to evaluate customer behavior, product contribution, geographical performance, revenue growth, and business trends.

The analysis focuses on identifying major revenue drivers, product performance patterns, customer purchasing behavior, and operational insights that can support strategic business decisions.

## Business Objectives

The project was designed to answer key business questions such as:

1. Profit trends over time
2. Revenue growth performance
3. Geographical sales contribution
4. Purchasing power by income level
5. Occupation-based purchasing behavior
6. Return rate impact on revenue
